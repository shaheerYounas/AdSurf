# User Personas

| Persona | Responsibilities | Needs | Risks to reduce |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Amazon seller owner | Owns product growth and ad spend | Simple workflow, explainable recommendations, approval control | Overspending, irrelevant keywords, confusing exports |
| Agency account manager | Manages multiple clients and products | Repeatable process, client-ready explanations, audit trail | Inconsistent campaign structures, missed approvals |
| PPC analyst | Reviews keyword quality and optimizations | Transparent scoring, performance metrics, override ability | Black-box AI recommendations |
| Operations admin | Manages users, roles, files, and governance | Workspace controls, security, logs, export history | Data leaks, accidental customer changes |

## Persona-Specific Acceptance
| Persona | Success signal |
| --- | --- |
| Seller owner | Can approve a campaign plan confidently without understanding raw bulk sheet mechanics. |
| Agency account manager | Can process multiple product profiles with consistent output and client approval evidence. |
| PPC analyst | Can inspect rule inputs, outputs, and explanations before approving changes. |
| Operations admin | Can trace who approved what, when, and from which source data. |
